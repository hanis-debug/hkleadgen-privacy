1. Introduction

HK Lead Gen ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our services, including our LinkedIn-integrated lead generation tools.

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Our application accesses LinkedIn data through LinkedIn's official API in compliance with the LinkedIn API Terms of Use. We only request permissions necessary for the services we provide. We do not scrape, store, or redistribute LinkedIn member data beyond what is required to fulfil your specific request.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
